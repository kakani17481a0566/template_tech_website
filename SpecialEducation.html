Special Education Needs

Informed, Individualised & Timely Decisions

Helping Educators With IEP And RTI Planning Through Genetic Insights And Continuous Cognitive Tracking.

NeuroPiTM is a neurogenetic and AI-powered learning system that empowers schools and specialists to streamline and strengthen the support for students with special educational needs. It helps educators identify, monitor, and support diverse learners with objective, real-time data on cognitive and behavioral development. It’s a powerful tool for executing both IEP (Individualized Education Program) and RTI (Response to Intervention) processes through evidence-based personalization.

🔧 For IEP Teams

NeuroPiTM helps Special Education teams by:

  • 📊 Providing objective, ongoing data for present levels of performance (PLOP)
  • 🎯 Recommending targeted accommodations based on cognitive profiles
  • 🧩 Supporting goal-setting with developmentally appropriate benchmarks
  • 📆 Tracking progress toward IEP goals in real time
  • 📄 Generating exportable reports for IEP documentation

🏁 For RTI Teams

  • 🚦 Automatically flagging students showing early signs of difficulty
  • 📈 Tracking response to specific interventions (e.g., working memory training)
  • 🧠 Providing brain-based rationale for movement across RTI tiers
  • 🛠️ Suggesting interventions tailored to a student’s unique cognitive and learning profile
  • ⏲️ Supporting frequent progress checks to adjust interventions quickly
