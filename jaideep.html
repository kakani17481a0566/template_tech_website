Mr. Jaideep Gaddam

The Chief Financial Officer

Strategic Financial Leadership & Governance

The Chief Financial Officer of NeuroPi is a Chartered Accountant with deep experience in institutional finance, capital structuring, and governance for high-growth technology and healthcare platforms. He brings a rare combination of Big Four training and hands-on operator exposure, positioning him as a capital steward across fundraising, scale, and eventual exit cycles.

He began his career at KPMG, where he worked on statutory audits, IPO-linked assignments, and financial reporting for listed and multinational groups, including environments governed by SOX and US GAAP–linked reporting. This foundation has shaped a rigorous approach to financial controls, investor-grade reporting, and board-level transparency.

Beyond assurance, he has led funding readiness and capital strategy for promoter-led and institutionally backed businesses, advising on valuation frameworks, cap-table architecture, ESOP design, dilution management, and working-capital optimization. He has worked closely with Founder and Senior Management on cash runway planning, burn discipline, and unit-economics tracking, ensuring growth is pursued with capital efficiency and downside protection.

At NeuroPi, he owns the end-to-end finance mandate — including capital allocation, fundraising support, investor communications, governance, and exit preparedness. He acts as a strategic partner to the founder and Board, ensuring that NeuroPi scales with financial discipline, defensible valuations, and institutional credibility, while protecting long-term equity value for shareholders.
