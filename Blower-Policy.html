    1. Purpose

    NeuroPi is committed to the highest standards of ethics, transparency, child safety, and governance.

    This Whistle Blower Policy enables any individual to safely report concerns related to misconduct, violations of law, unethical behaviour, child protection issues, financial irregularities, or breaches of NeuroPi's Code of Conduct.

    The purpose of this policy is to:

    • Encourage timely reporting of genuine concerns
    • Ensure protection of whistle blowers
    • Enable independent investigation
    • Maintain NeuroPi as a safe ecosystem for children, staff, and stakeholders

    2. Scope

    This policy applies to:

    • Employees (full-time, part-time, contractual)
    • Teachers & school staff
    • Franchise partners & operators
    • Vendors & consultants
    • Parents, guardians, and community members
    • Volunteers & interns

    Covers all NeuroPi verticals including Schools, Early Development Programs, NeuroPi Sport, NeuroPi Longevity, AI/Tech platforms, Clinic-in-a-Bag, and Operations.

    3. What Can Be Reported

    Whistleblowers may report concerns including (but not limited to):

    A. Child Safety and Well-being

    • Neglect, discrimination, harassment, or abuse
    • Breach of child-safety protocols
    • Unsafe learning or physical environments

    B. Ethical Misconduct

    • Harassment, discrimination, bullying
    • Abuse of authority
    • Breach of confidentiality

    C. Financial & Operational Misconduct

    • Fraud, corruption, misappropriation
    • Bribery or kickbacks
    • Manipulation of school records or financial statements

    D. Compliance Violations

    • Violations of NEP 2020, RTE Act, or any applicable law
    • Breach of licensing, franchising, or operational guidelines
    • Cybersecurity violations, data privacy breaches

    E. Academic & Scientific Integrity

    • Misuse of NeuroPi pedagogy or AI systems
    • Fabrication or manipulation of assessments or analytics

    4. Reporting Channels

    NeuroPi provides multiple safe and confidential channels:

    Email: whistleblower.neuropi@gmail.com

    Hotline: Anonymous call number +39 3883883802

    Online Form: Secure form on the NeuroPi website

    Physical Drop Box: Sealed boxes at NeuroPi Schools & regional offices

    All reports may be anonymous unless law requires identity disclosure.

    5. Confidentiality & Protection

    NeuroPi guarantees:

    • Absolute confidentiality of whistle blower identity
    • Zero retaliation policy
    • Protection against dismissal, demotion, harassment, or discrimination
    • Immediate action against retaliators
    • Optional anonymity through encrypted reporting

    6. Investigation Process

    1. Acknowledge receipt (within 48 hours)
    2. Preliminary assessment
    3. Independent investigation by Internal Ethics Committee
    4. Findings & recommendations documented transparently
    5. Corrective action (disciplinary, legal, policy adjustments)
    6. Closure notification (when permissible)

    In case of child safety issues, NeuroPi will immediately involve:

    • Local authorities (as mandated)
    • Child helpline services
    • Regulatory bodies

    7. Roles & Responsibilities

    Whistleblower

    • Provide genuine, factual information
    • Not misuse the policy for false allegations

    Internal Ethics Committee

    • Ensure fair, unbiased investigations
    • Maintain confidentiality
    • Recommend action

    Management

    • Protect whistleblowers
    • Execute corrective measures
    • Strengthen systems and controls

    8. Frivolous Complaints

    False, malicious, or knowingly misleading complaints may result in disciplinary action — without discouraging genuine reporting.

    9. Policy Review

    This policy will be reviewed annually or upon major expansion of NeuroPi verticals to ensure continued relevance and compliance.

    NeuroPi Stance

    NeuroPi stands for transparency, child safety, and ethical excellence. This policy creates a culture where every voice matters and every concern is acted upon.
