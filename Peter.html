DR. DR. PETER GSELLER

Founding Investor

"Guiding The Human Potential"

Since the beginning of my professional career, I have always taken an unconventional approach and thought in terms of a sustainable vision. My multi-talented career started with a Big Four accounting firm where I accompanied M&A transactions as a management consultant before joining top-tier US law firms advising on private equity and venture capital transactions.

I have further served (and still serve) on various boards of DACH-based multi-family offices, managing and overseeing venture capital as well as private equity investments and scaling startups. Today, I invest, alongside the biggest names in US Tech, in several technology and ESG-focused ventures that drive foremost social but also environmental impact.

I strongly believe that businesses can create value and change the world simultaneously. As a lifelong learner, I trust in education as the foundation of society and the path to sustainable development calling it "LONGCIETY".
