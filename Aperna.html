Dr. Aperna Volluru

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

A Scientist, Technology Innovator, and Industrial Intelligence Visionary with Over 30 Years of International Experience in Artificial Intelligence, Enterprise Systems, Human Intelligence, Data Science, and Digital Transformation

As the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of NeuroPi Tech Pvt. Ltd., Dr. Aperna Volluru brings more than 30 years of international experience spanning Artificial Intelligence, Enterprise Information Systems, Data Science, Neuroscience, Human Intelligence, Digital Transformation, and Technology Innovation.
