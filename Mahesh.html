Mr. Uma Mahesh

The Chief Marketing Officer

A Spirited Social Entrepreneur with 25 Years of Experience

The Chief Marketing Officer of NeuroPi brings over 25 years of experience defined by a blend of innovative marketing strategies, business acumen, and an unwavering belief in the power of technology to improve lives. Deeply involved in the wellness tech space, he understands how to position cutting-edge products to meet the needs of modern consumers—from consumer behavior insights to customer engagement strategies. At NeuroPi, his role is to lead marketing efforts and communicate the vision of human performance optimization to audiences across education, wellness, sports, and defense.

He has extensive experience in technology and research, product development, project implementations, and business consultations. With a track record of successfully launching and scaling wellness-focused technology products, he is passionate about leveraging his marketing expertise to promote NeuroPi's mission to optimize human potential through genetics, neuroscience, and AI.

Key Experience and Expertise

  • Social Entrepreneurship in Wellness Tech: He has founded and scaled multiple businesses in the wellness technology space. These ventures range from fitness wearables and healthcare IoT applications to AI-driven wellness platforms, where he led marketing and sales strategies that drove growth and market penetration.
  • Marketing Strategy & Brand Development: With a deep understanding of both traditional and digital marketing, he has crafted and executed multi-channel campaigns that built strong brands and captured market share. His expertise includes executing Government IT projects, brand positioning, customer segmentation, and content strategy.
  • Sales Leadership & Consumer Engagement: He has successfully led sales teams and developed consumer engagement strategies for products focused on health and well-being—especially in aviation safety, industrial fire & safety, logistics & fleet management, and energy.
  • Data-Driven Marketing & Growth: He strongly advocates for data-driven insights to inform marketing decisions. From market research to analytics, he uses technology to understand and predict consumer behavior, ensuring alignment with demand and customer expectations.

Prestigious Projects & Clientele

He has had the privilege of working on projects for the Government of Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Meghalaya, Jharkhand, Nagaland, Goa, and Kerala; private sector giants like Reliance, Vedanta, Jindal Steel Works, and Kakinada Port; and in defense & civil aviation, including CIAL (Cochin International Airport).

Awards & Recognition

Over the years, he has received several awards for marketing leadership in the Wellness Tech industry. The most prestigious was being felicitated by the A.P. Differently Abled & Senior Citizens Assistance Corporation on World Braille Day for contributions to healthcare services for blind children.
