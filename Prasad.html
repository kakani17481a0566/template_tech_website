Mr. Prasad Garapati

The Chairman

A Distinguished Technology Industry Leader and Serial Entrepreneur with Over 45 Years of Leadership in Technology, Digital Transformation, Manufacturing, International Business, Government Advisory, and Innovation Ecosystems

As Chairman of NeuroPi, Mr. Prasad Garapati brings over 45 years of distinguished leadership in technology, manufacturing, entrepreneurship, international business, digital transformation, and innovation-led enterprise development. Throughout his career, he has successfully founded, scaled, and advised numerous technology-driven businesses while actively contributing to India's information technology ecosystem through leadership roles with government, industry associations, and international technology initiatives.

His strategic vision has consistently focused on building globally competitive technology enterprises, strengthening India's digital economy, fostering international collaborations, and accelerating the adoption of emerging technologies across industry. At NeuroPi, he provides strategic leadership, corporate governance, industry partnerships, international outreach, and long-term technology commercialization, ensuring that the NeuroPi Industrial Intelligence Platform (NIIP) evolves as a globally scalable engineering intelligence platform for defence and strategic industries.

Technology Industry Leadership

Mr. Garapati has spent more than four decades building technology enterprises, promoting innovation, and supporting the growth of India's software and electronics industry. His entrepreneurial journey spans technology, manufacturing, software services, business transformation, agriculture, education, media, and emerging technologies, providing him with extensive experience in organizational leadership, technology commercialization, strategic planning, and international business development.

His ability to transform innovative technologies into commercially viable enterprises provides NeuroPi with strong strategic direction for long-term growth and sustainable technology development.

National Leadership in India's Information Technology Sector

Mr. Garapati served as Chairman of the Electronics and Computer Software Export Promotion Council (ESC), an autonomous organization sponsored by the Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India, responsible for promoting India's electronics and software exports worldwide.

During his leadership:

  • India's software and electronics export ecosystem expanded significantly.
  • ESC strengthened its international presence through global technology partnerships.
  • Numerous Indian technology companies were connected with international customers and governments.
  • India's technology capabilities were promoted across major international markets.
  • Global technology collaboration and export opportunities were significantly expanded.

His leadership contributed to strengthening India's position as a trusted global technology partner.
