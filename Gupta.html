Mr. MNR Gupta, M.Tech., MBA

Chief Operating Officer (COO)

A Global Infrastructure and Operations Leader with Over 30 Years of International Experience in Large-Scale Infrastructure Delivery, Engineering Programme Management, Strategic Operations, and Enterprise Transformation

As the Chief Operating Officer (COO) of NeuroPi, Mr. MNR Gupta brings over 30 years of executive leadership in delivering large-scale infrastructure programmes, engineering operations, strategic planning, and organizational transformation across international markets. His distinguished career has been built upon successfully managing some of the Middle East's most prestigious infrastructure developments, including the world-renowned Sohar Port and Salalah Port projects in the Sultanate of Oman. These projects collectively represented infrastructure programmes valued at more than USD 46 billion, where he played a significant leadership role in planning, execution, stakeholder coordination, quality assurance, safety, and operational excellence.

His expertise spans programme management, infrastructure engineering, integrated transportation planning, logistics, operations management, enterprise governance, business transformation, and organizational leadership. Throughout his career, he has consistently delivered complex engineering projects within demanding international environments while maintaining world-class standards for quality, safety, schedule, and operational performance.

At NeuroPi, Mr. Gupta leads programme governance, operational strategy, project execution, stakeholder engagement, quality assurance, and organizational readiness, ensuring that the NeuroPi Industrial Intelligence Platform (NIIP) is delivered using globally recognized engineering and project management practices suitable for defence and mission-critical industries.
